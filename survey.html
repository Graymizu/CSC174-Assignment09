A Cat Breed Catastrophe

A pet or companion animal is an animal kept primarily for a person's company, protection, or entertainment rather than as a working animal, livestock, or laboratory animal. Popular pets are often noted for their attractive appearances, and their loyal or playful personalities. Pets provide their owners (or guardians) physical and emotional benefits.

A cat is regarded as many to be the premiere companion; they are low maintenance, fun to snuggle, and catch mice in your house. There are numerous debates over which cat breed is in fact the American favorite. Is it the American Short-hair? The Maine Coone? The Bengal? This survey aims to understand how many pets our users currently possess and of what kind.
